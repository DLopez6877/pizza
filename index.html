About Gotham Pizza

Batman eats here. True story. One time a passer-by was walking on the street and two muggers held a gun up to her head and told her to give them all her money. Suddenly... BAM!! Batman swung down from a building (practically out of nowhere) and kicked the muggers through the front window. Before you could blink an eye, Batman already had the robbers tied up. He walked up to cashier working that day and handed him $1000. Batman said, "This is for the window and a slice of pepperoni." Batman waited for the slice. After he ate it he said, "Call the cops. Also that pizza is amazing. I'll be back."

We believe he still eats here today when he isn't in disquise.
